    How to Play

    Use your arrow keys or swipe to move the tiles. When two tiles with the same shape touch, they merge into the next shape!

    Reach the MindMerge tile to win the game. Try to get the highest score! Scores are recorded on restart or gameover.

    NEW RULE: When two MindMerge tiles merge, they create a new random shape (Circle, Square, Triangle, Star, or Diamond) to keep the game going!
